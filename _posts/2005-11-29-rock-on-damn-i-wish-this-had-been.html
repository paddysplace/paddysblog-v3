Damn I wish this had been around when I was a teen date: '2005-11-29T19:41:00.000Z' author: Paddy tags: modified_time: '2005-11-29T19:42:45.603Z' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-17598736.post-113329336560155964 blogger_orig_url: https://paddeesplace.blogspot.com/2005/11/rock-on-damn-i-wish-this-had-been.html --- Aspiring rock gods can at last create their own guitar solos - without ever having to pick up a real instrument, thanks to a group of Finnish computer science students.

The Virtual Air Guitar project, developed at the Helsinki University of Technology, adds genuine electric guitar sounds to the passionately played air guitar.

Using a computer to monitor the hand movements of a "player", the system adds riffs and licks to match frantic mid-air finger work. By responding instantly to a wide variety of gestures it promises to turn even the least musically gifted air guitarist to a virtual fret board virtuoso.
